# Research: Features

## Table Stakes (Must Have)
- **High-Definition Output**: Minimum 4K resolution exports to communicate premium artistic quality.
- **Mathematical Accuracy**: The visual dimensions, colors, or particle positions must map explicitly to the mathematical output, not pseudo-random approximations.
- **Web Gallery Interface**: A smooth digital exhibition space optimized for viewing large video assets.
- **Methodology Documentation**: White paper formatting for mathematical transparency alongside the art.

## Differentiators (Competitive Advantage)
- **Proof-to-Art Pipeline**: Specific features showing step-by-step visualizations of equations (e.g., golden ratio rectangles naturally emerging from Fibonacci spirals).
- **Abstract Infinity Concept**: Visually unbounded structures (like Mandelbrot zooms with Python-driven precision bounds).

## Anti-Features (What to avoid)
- **Real-time 3D Interactivity**: Given the computation depth, allowing users to orbit the visualizer in real-time will sacrifice visual fidelity or require excessive optimization. Keep the gallery video-centric for perfect presentation.
